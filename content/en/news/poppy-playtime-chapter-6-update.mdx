Unlike the standard toys, Lily appears to be an amalgamation of parts, featuring elongated, braided limbs that allow her to reach farther and hold tighter.

The narrative seems to be shifting toward the Prototype’s endgame. We have seen the Prototype collecting the remains of fallen toys—like CatNap and Mommy Long Legs—to build a singular, massive entity. Chapter 6 is expected to delve into the "why" behind these experiments.
