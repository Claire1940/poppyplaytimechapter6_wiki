## How to Access Restricted Hidden Rooms

Accessing the most protected **poppy playtime chapter 6 hidden rooms** requires mastering the new "Kinetic Pull" mechanic. This allows you to interact with heavy objects from a distance, often revealing crawlspaces hidden behind industrial machinery.

### Step-by-Step Guide to the "Lemon Squad" Room
1. **Locate the Maintenance Hall:** Proceed past the initial chase sequence until you reach the fork in the path.
2. **Identify the Cracked Wall:** Look for a wall with yellow "caution" tape that appears slightly recessed.
3. **Use the Kinetic Pull:** Aim your GrabPack at the loose pipe above the wall and pull downward to cause a structural collapse.
4. **Enter the Crawlspace:** Squat and move through the opening to find the Lemon Squad—Chumbo, Smack Shaft, Pointman, and Torch Boy.

> **Tip:** Don't forget to press the buttons on each member of the Lemon Squad. They provide a synchronized dialogue sequence that reveals the location of a hidden VHS tape.

## FAQ

**Q: Are the poppy playtime chapter 6 hidden rooms required for the main ending?**

A: While you can complete the main story without entering every hidden room, you will miss significant portions of the lore and the "True Ending" sequence which requires collecting all hidden VHS tapes found in these rooms.

**Q: How do I find the hidden brother cutout?**

A: The "Secret Brother" cutout is located in Lily’s Secret Sanctuary. You must find a small ventilation shaft near the ceiling in the Toy Storage area and use your GrabPack to pull yourself up. It is widely considered one of the hardest **poppy playtime chapter 6 hidden rooms** to locate.

**Q: Is the Lemon Squad dangerous?**

A: In the current version of Chapter 6, the Lemon Squad exists only as cardboard cutouts and environmental storytelling. However, lore notes found nearby suggest they were being developed as physical entities before the factory's closure.

**Q: Can I go back to find missed rooms after finishing the chapter?**

A: Yes, the 2026 version of the game includes a "Chapter Select" and "Encounter Select" menu that allows you to revisit specific sections of the factory to hunt for any secrets or cutouts you may have missed on your first playthrough.
