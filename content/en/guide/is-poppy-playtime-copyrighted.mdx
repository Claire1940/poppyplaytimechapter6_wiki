**Q: Can I use Poppy Playtime characters in my YouTube thumbnails?**

**Q: Is it legal to make a Poppy Playtime fan game on Roblox?**

A: Roblox has specific policies regarding IP. While many fan games exist, Mob Entertainment has the right to request their removal if the game uses "ripped" assets from the original files or if the game is monetized heavily without permission.

**Q: Does Mob Entertainment own the rights to the name "Huggy Wuggy"?**

A: While "Huggy Wuggy" is a descriptive name, Mob Entertainment holds the trademark for the name as it pertains to toys, video games, and entertainment media. This means you cannot start a toy company and name a blue monster "Huggy Wuggy."

**Q: Is Poppy Playtime copyrighted if I only use the music?**

A: Yes, the musical score is a copyrighted work. If you use the music without the context of playing the game, you may receive a copyright claim on platforms like YouTube or Twitch. Always try to use the music as part of a transformative video.
