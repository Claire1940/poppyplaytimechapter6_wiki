## The Prototype’s "Tea Party" and Lillian’s Identity

During the final encounter, the Prototype taunts the player with a chilling description of a "Tea Party." This isn't just flavor text; it refers to the systematic collection of "guests" (other toys and humans) he has integrated into his mass. His claim that "everything here belongs to me" highlights his god-complex within the abandoned factory.

The mention of Lillian is perhaps the biggest lore drop in the **poppy playtime chapter 6 ending**. The Prototype claims she is "not yours" but rather "his concern," suggesting that the human souls trapped within the toys are being fought over like property. This revelation points toward a deeper connection between the staff of Playtime Co. and the current monstrosities roaming the halls.

## How to Trigger the Final Sequence

To successfully reach the **poppy playtime chapter 6 ending**, players must complete a series of environmental puzzles that lead to the Stage Rig. Follow these steps to ensure you don't miss the climax:

1.  **Locate Locker C9:** In the Cold Storage area, find the case inside Locker C9. This contains the necessary components for the final GrabPack upgrade.
2.  **The Utility Room Shutoff:** Once the Prototype appears, ignore his initial taunts and head directly to the utility room. You must find the water shutoff valve to prep the sprinklers.
3.  **The High-Voltage Lever:** After the room is flooded, climb the ladders to the second-floor rig. Avoid the Prototype's reaching claws as the platform is unstable.
4.  **The Final Drop:** Wait for the prompt when the Prototype is standing directly in the center of the flooded stage. Release the cable to trigger the ending cinematic.

> **💡 Tip:** Don't spend too much time looking at the Prototype during the flooding phase. The rising water level makes the floor slippery, and falling can lead to an instant "Game Over."
