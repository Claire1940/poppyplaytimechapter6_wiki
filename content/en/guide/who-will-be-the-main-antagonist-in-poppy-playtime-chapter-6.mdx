This would set up a three-way conflict where the player is caught between a mad scientist and a vengeful god-like entity.

## New Experiments: The Mystery of 1268 and 1424

Mob Entertainment often teases future villains through VHS tapes and documents found in earlier chapters. In the labs of Chapter 5, players discovered notes regarding **Experiment 1268**, a male subject who underwent intense conditioning involving "Poppy Gel."

### Experiment 1268 (Lewis Lucy)
According to the logs, this subject was conditioned for over 14 hours until he began singing and tapping his foot, repeating the phrase "Joy, I live it." This successful conditioning suggests a loyal, highly dangerous soldier for the Prototype—or perhaps a failed experiment that has been stewing in the lower levels for decades.

### Experiment 1424
Mentioned as a "failure" kept within the prison sector, 1424 represents the darker side of Playtime Co.’s history. If Chapter 6 takes us into the deeper caverns or the rumored "Hidden City" beneath the factory, these forgotten failures could serve as the primary obstacles.
