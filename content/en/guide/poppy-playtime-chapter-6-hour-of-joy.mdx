## Key Antagonists in Chapter 6

The primary boss encounter in this chapter involves a massive, multi-limbed entity that guards the entrance to the factory's primary power core. Defeating this creature requires the player to synchronize the GrabPack's electrical charges with the factory's failing grid, a puzzle that spans multiple floors.

> **💡 Tip:** During the Power Core boss fight, always keep one hand attached to a green power outlet to maintain a constant charge for your Sonic Pulse Hand.
