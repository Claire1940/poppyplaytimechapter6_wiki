## Navigating the Utility Room and Fire Systems

Restoring the water flow is a mandatory step in the Chapter 6 progression. The Utility Room is located on the basement level of the theater. Follow these steps to successfully navigate this section of the **poppy playtime chapter 6 map**:

1.  **Locate the Shutoff Valve:** Enter the Utility Room through the vent near the stage left entrance.
2.  **Clear the Obstruction:** Use the GrabPack to pull the debris away from the main pipe.
3.  **Restore Power:** If the system is dead, you must head to the second floor to pull the emergency lever.
4.  **Activate Sprinklers:** Once the water pressure is stabilized, return to the stage control booth to prime the fire system.

## Advanced Movement: The Second Floor Catwalks

Verticality plays a massive role in the **poppy playtime chapter 6 map**. Many players find themselves stuck when the ground-level doors are locked. The catwalks provide a secondary path through the facility, but they are fraught with danger. 

The Prototype often attempts to knock the player off these narrow paths. It is recommended to keep your GrabPack hands attached to the safety rails whenever possible to maintain balance. This area also contains several hidden VHS tapes that provide lore about Doctor Sawyer and the creation of Experiment 1006.

> **Tip:** If you lose your way, look for the yellow painted pipes. These pipes always lead toward a progression-related lever or door.

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the Locker C9 key located on the poppy playtime chapter 6 map?**

A: The key is not in Cold Storage itself. You must find it in the Maintenance Office located near the Utility Room entrance. Once you have the key, backtrack to Cold Storage to open the locker.

**Q: How do I fix the "No Water" error during the stage sequence?**

A: If there is no water, you need to check the Utility Room for a shutoff. If the power is also out, you must climb to the second floor and pull the emergency lever to restart the facility's pumps.

**Q: Can Huggy Wuggy help me navigate the map?**

A: In Chapter 6, Huggy and Rory act as allies in specific scripted sequences. While they don't give you a physical map, following their movements often leads you toward the next objective during the final boss fight.

**Q: Is the poppy playtime chapter 6 map larger than Chapter 3?**

A: While Chapter 3 had more open-world elements, Chapter 6 features more densely packed rooms and multiple floors. The total navigable square footage is roughly 20% larger, but it feels more compact due to the indoor setting.

**Q: What happens if I fall off the catwalks?**

A: Falling from the catwalks usually results in immediate death or a reset to the last checkpoint. Use the GrabPack's magnetic grip to stay secured to the railings during the Prototype's "shaking" attacks.
