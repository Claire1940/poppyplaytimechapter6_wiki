## Poppy Playtime Chapter 6 Gameplay Overview

Poppy Playtime is a survival horror puzzle game developed by Mob Entertainment. Each chapter builds upon the previous one with new gameplay mechanics, new environments, and escalating horror.

## Core Gameplay Systems

### The GrabPack

The GrabPack is the player's primary tool across all chapters. It allows:

- Grabbing objects from a distance with the blue hand
- Generating electricity to power circuits with the red hand
- Pulling platforms, doors, and environmental elements
- Launching yourself across gaps using grapple mechanics

### Puzzle Design Philosophy

Poppy Playtime puzzles typically involve:

1. **Observation** — Scanning the environment for clues and mechanisms
2. **Experimentation** — Testing interactions with the GrabPack
3. **Timing** — Many puzzles require precise timing under pressure
4. **Logic chains** — Multi-step solutions that require completing parts in order

### Enemy Encounters

Chapter 6 is expected to introduce new enemies while potentially bringing back familiar threats. Enemy encounter types include:

- **Chase sequences** — Run from a pursuing enemy in timed escape routes
- **Stealth sections** — Avoid detection while solving puzzles
- **Boss fights** — Multi-phase encounters against major antagonists

## Graphics and Audio

Poppy Playtime uses Unreal Engine, delivering:

- High-fidelity horror environments
- Dynamic lighting and shadow effects
- Atmospheric sound design that evolves with tension
- Detailed character models for both allies and enemies
