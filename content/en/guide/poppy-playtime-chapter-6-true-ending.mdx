## The Final Boss: Mechanics and Strategy

The final encounter in Chapter 6 is a multi-stage battle that tests your mastery of the GrabPack and your ability to manage environmental hazards. The boss, a culmination of several failed experiments, utilizes a "Heat" mechanic that can quickly overwhelm the player if not managed correctly.

During the initial phase, the boss will attempt to control the room's temperature. You will hear repeated mechanical cues—often sounding like "Heat, Heat"—indicating that the floor grates are about to become lethal. You must use your GrabPack to swing to the overhead pipes to avoid damage.

### Stage 2: The Red Smoke Hallucination
Once the boss's health reaches 50%, the room fills with red smoke. If you haven't neutralized the smoke using the filtration system mentioned in the table above, the boss will become invisible, and you will have to rely on audio cues to dodge attacks.

Instead of attacking the boss directly, you must reroute the coolant pipes to the central mirror. This reflects the boss's energy back at itself, revealing its true form and triggering the final cinematic.
