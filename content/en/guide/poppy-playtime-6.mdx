## Advanced Gameplay Mechanics: Elemental Combat

In **poppy playtime 6**, the environmental puzzles have evolved from simple circuit-flipping to complex, multi-stage traps designed to exploit the physical weaknesses of the larger monsters. The Prototype, being comprised of a significant amount of metallic components, is particularly vulnerable to electrical conductivity. 

Players must master the "Fry and Flood" tactic. This involves activating the factory's ancient fire suppression systems to soak the combat arena, followed by the deployment of high-voltage cables from the stage rigging. This synergy between water and electricity represents a significant step up in the series' mechanical complexity.

### Steps to Execute the High-Voltage Trap

1.  **Locate the Utility Room:** Find the main water shut-off valve to prime the sprinkler system.
2.  **Restore Emergency Power:** Navigate to the second floor to pull the emergency lever when the main grid fails.
3.  **Deploy Stage Cables:** Ascend the stage riggings to drop live high-voltage lines into the flooded zone.
4.  **Lure the Beast:** Keep the Prototype positioned within the wet zone until the circuit is completed.

> **Warning:** The stage riggings are structurally unstable. Avoid staying in one place for too long, as the heights in the theater area are lethal if you slip.
