## Detailed Walkthrough: The Lower Laboratories

The journey through the lower laboratories is divided into four distinct sub-zones. Each zone challenges a different aspect of the player's skills, from stealth to high-speed platforming.

### Zone 1: The Incubation Chambers
This is where the earliest experiments were kept. You will find rows of glass vats, some containing recognizable toy parts. Your goal here is to restore power to the main lift while avoiding the "Surgical Assistants"—small, spider-like toys that move only when you aren't looking at them.

### Zone 2: The Formula Archives
This area is a massive library of biological data. Here, the **poppy playtime wiki chapter 6** community has discovered that the puzzles rely heavily on the "numbers in a formula" mentioned by Sawyer. You must collect three missing data discs to proceed.

1.  **Locate the Red Disc** in the restricted records room.
2.  **Decode the Blue Disc** using the frequency modulator near the central terminal.
3.  **Retrieve the Green Disc** from the incinerator room (requires a timed puzzle).

### Zone 3: The Doctor’s Theater
This is a scripted encounter where Sawyer forces you to participate in a series of "efficiency tests." You must complete three rooms of puzzles while he mocks your intelligence over the loudspeaker. 

> **💡 Tip:** Always keep your back to the wall during the Theater segment. Sawyer's assistants often spawn behind the player during the puzzle-solving phase.

## Boss Fight Strategy: Outsmarting the Genius

The final encounter in Chapter 6 isn't a traditional chase. Instead, it is a multi-stage puzzle boss fight against a massive, mechanized version of Sawyer’s "Perfect Formula."

### Stage 1: The Calibration
Sawyer will flood the room with gas. You must use the GrabPack to pull four levers in the corners of the room to activate the ventilation system. Watch the floor for shifting tiles that reveal spikes.

### Stage 2: The Linguistic Lock
The monitor in the center of the room will display a series of symbols. You must match these symbols to the corresponding buttons on your Frequency Modulator. Each mistake causes Sawyer to release a "Surgical Assistant" into the arena.

### Stage 3: The Final Formula
In the final phase, you must redirect the power from the boss's life support system into the main door. This requires linking three electrical nodes while dodging projectiles. Once the power reaches 100%, the boss will overheat, allowing you to escape into the elevator.

## Survival Tips for Chapter 6

*   **Listen Closely:** The audio cues in Chapter 6 are more important than ever. The hum of a machine can indicate a nearby secret or an approaching enemy.
*   **Conserve Power:** The Frequency Modulator has a limited battery that recharges slowly. Don't waste it on non-essential electronics.
*   **Check the Ceilings:** Many of the new enemies in the laboratory sections prefer to drop from above rather than chase from behind.
*   **Read the Notes:** The scattered papers on desks often contain the solutions to the "Formula" puzzles found later in the level.
