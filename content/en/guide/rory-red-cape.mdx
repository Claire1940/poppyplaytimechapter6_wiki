## Surviving the "Red Light" Sequence

One of the most intense moments involving the **rory red cape** entity is the "Red Light, Green Light" challenge. This sequence requires the player to move only when the entity is singing. 

1.  **Listen for the Jingle:** The entity will sing a distorted version of the Rory Redcape theme.
2.  **Freeze on Silence:** The moment the singing stops, you must cease all movement, including camera rotation.
3.  **Find Cover:** Use the environment to break line-of-sight between song intervals.
4.  **Reach the Prize:** The goal is to follow the path to the "prize" (often a key item or a door release).

If you fail to freeze, the braided limbs will descend from the ceiling, leading to an immediate game-over. The entity mocks the player's "hiding and cheating," suggesting it has a heightened sense of awareness compared to earlier bosses like Huggy Wuggy.

## FAQ

**Q: Is Rory Red Cape a hero or a villain in Chapter 6?**

A: While the original toy was marketed as a hero, the version encountered in Chapter 6 is an antagonist. The Prototype has transformed him into a "braided" monster that serves as a guardian of the house stage.

**Q: How do you track Rory Red Cape during the house sequence?**

A: Players must look for "cookie crumbs" on the floor. These are pieces of the character's shield that shed as he moves. Following these crumbs will lead you to the side entrance and eventually to the encounter with Lily.

**Q: What happens if you move during the "Red Light" phase?**

A: If you move while the entity is silent, you will be caught by the braided limbs. This results in an immediate failure of the sequence. You must stay "perfectly, perfectly still" until the singing resumes.

**Q: Can the Cookie Shield be used by the player?**

A: In the current 2026 build, the shield is primarily an environmental object and a tracking tool used by the antagonist. However, certain puzzle segments require the player to interact with shield fragments to trigger door sensors.
