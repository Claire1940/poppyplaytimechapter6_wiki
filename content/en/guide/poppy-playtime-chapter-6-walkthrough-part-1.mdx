These labs were once the site of the most classified projects at Playtime Co., and the architecture reflects a shift from corporate whimsy to industrial dread.

In this section of the **poppy playtime chapter 6 walkthrough part 1**, your primary objective is to restore power to the primary elevator shaft. The elevator is the only way to reach the "Mirror Rooms" mentioned in the recovered doctor's logs.

## Surviving the Heat Mechanics

A new mechanic introduced in this chapter is the concept of "Super Heat." Certain objects and enemies emit an intense thermal signature that can damage the player or disable their tools. Following this **poppy playtime chapter 6 walkthrough part 1** requires you to learn how to vent this heat into the environment.

When you hear the "Heat. Heat." audio cue, it indicates that a thermal vent is about to erupt. You must find a cooling station or use your GrabPack to pull a release valve. These valves are often located high on the walls, requiring a quick reach and pull.

1.  **Identify the Vent:** Look for steam or red glowing pipes.
2.  **Locate the Valve:** Usually connected via a yellow wire to the vent.
3.  **Deploy GrabPack:** Use the Blue hand to latch onto the valve.
4.  **Hold and Release:** Wait for the thermal gauge to drop before letting go.

## Encountering the "Baby" Experiment

Toward the end of the first part of the walkthrough, you will encounter a creature referred to in the logs as "the baby." This is not a human infant, but a twisted, early-stage experiment that the doctor believed was dead. This encounter is a stealth-based sequence where you must use the shadows and the Red Smoke to stay out of sight.

The creature reacts to sound and heat. If your GrabPack is overheating, the "baby" will track you instantly. You must navigate a series of rusted catwalks while managing your thermal output. This segment is the climax of the **poppy playtime chapter 6 walkthrough part 1** and requires the most focus.

### Stealth Sequence Checklist
*   Check your GrabPack temperature before moving.
*   Crouch-walk to minimize noise on metal surfaces.
*   Wait for the "Heat" audio cue to mask your footsteps.
*   Use the "Mirror" shards on the floor to look around corners without exposing yourself.
