## VHS Tape Locations: Uncovering the Lore

VHS tapes remain the most vital **poppy playtime chapter 6 collectibles** for fans who want to piece together the story. In this chapter, the tapes focus heavily on the transition between Elliot Ludwig’s original vision and the horrific experiments that led to the creation of the Prototype.

1.  **The "Initial Intake" Tape (Blue):** Found in the very first security room after entering the Neural Labs. It is sitting on a desk partially obscured by a fallen monitor.
2.  **The "Experiment 1006: Neural Link" Tape (Red):** Located in the Observation Room overlooking the primary test chamber. You must use the Purple Hand to launch yourself to the upper catwalk to reach the player.
3.  **The "Ollie’s Message" Tape (Green):** This is found inside the ventilation shafts during the first major stealth sequence. Look for a side path that appears to be a dead end; the tape is resting near a small pile of purple fur.
4.  **The "Final Warning" Tape (Black):** Hidden behind a secret panel in the Director’s Office. To open it, you must interact with the books on the shelf in the order: Blue, Red, Yellow, Green.

## Golden Trophy Locations

For those aiming for the "Master Collector" achievement, the Golden Trophies are the ultimate prize. These are often hidden in areas that require backtracking or solving non-essential puzzles. Finding these **poppy playtime chapter 6 collectibles** often involves interacting with the environment in ways the game doesn't explicitly teach you.

### 1. The Golden Prototype Claw
After the first encounter with the main antagonist, return to the "Sorting Room." Use your GrabPack to pull a heavy crate away from the wall in the back-left corner. A small crawlspace will reveal the trophy sitting on a pedestal.

### 2. The Golden Poppy Flower
In the "Botanical Wing," you will find a series of light puzzles. Instead of completing the puzzle to open the main door, redirect the power to the small side-gate labeled "Storage B." Inside, the Golden Poppy is hidden among the overgrown vines.

### 3. The Golden GrabPack
During the "Maintenance Lift" sequence, look up as the elevator descends. There is a small ledge halfway down. You must time a jump or use the Grapple Hand to pull yourself onto the ledge before the lift passes it.

> **⚠️ Warning:** If you miss the ledge during the Maintenance Lift sequence, you cannot go back up without restarting the checkpoint. Ensure you are positioned on the front-right corner of the lift for the best view.

## FAQ

**Q: Are there any missable poppy playtime chapter 6 collectibles?**

A: Yes, several items in the "Chase Sequences" cannot be collected once the area is locked off. Specifically, the Golden GrabPack on the Maintenance Lift is the most commonly missed item.

**Q: How many VHS tapes are needed to understand the ending?**

A: While you can finish the game without any tapes, the "Final Warning" and "Ollie's Message" tapes are essential for understanding the true motivations of the Prototype and Poppy's relationship to the factory.

**Q: Do the collectibles carry over to a New Game Plus?**

A: Yes, once collected, trophies and tapes will appear in your "Collection Room" in the main menu, allowing you to track your progress across multiple playthroughs in 2026.

**Q: What happens if I find all the Hand Scanners?**

A: Finding and activating all the secret Hand Scanners unlocks a hidden room in the final area of the chapter, which contains a tribute to the developers and a teaser for the next installment in the franchise.
