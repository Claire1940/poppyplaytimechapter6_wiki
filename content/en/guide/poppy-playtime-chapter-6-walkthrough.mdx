## Starting the Rescue: Escaping the Infirmary

Chapter 6 begins immediately where Chapter 5 left off. You wake up in a makeshift infirmary, having been saved by Giblet after the Prototype’s attack. Your first objective is to regain your mobility and recover your GrabPack, which was damaged during the struggle.

The infirmary section acts as a soft tutorial for the new "Stealth Crawl" mechanic. Because your character is still recovering from a puncture wound, you cannot run for the first ten minutes of the game. You must use the shadows to avoid the "Scrap-Toys"—failed experiments that the Prototype has discarded but still roam the halls as sentries.

> **💡 Pro-Tip:** Do not attempt to engage the Scrap-Toys. Your current GrabPack is missing the shock module, meaning you have no way to stun enemies until you reach the Maintenance Wing.

## New Mechanics: The Bio-Link GrabPack

A major addition detailed in this **poppy playtime chapter 6 walkthrough** is the Bio-Link upgrade. Found in the R&D section of the lower factory, this upgrade allows your GrabPack to interface with organic-toy tissue. This is essential for opening "Flesh Gates" and reviving dormant machinery powered by the factory's biological core.

### How to Use the Bio-Link:
1.  **Identify a Bio-Port:** These look like glowing, pulsating sockets on walls or doors.
2.  **Connect the Green Hand:** The Green Hand now acts as a conductor for biological energy.
3.  **Pulse Management:** You must time your clicks with the "heartbeat" of the machine to successfully transfer power without causing a feedback loop that alerts nearby enemies.

## Puzzle Walkthrough: The Neural Network Room

One of the most difficult segments in the **poppy playtime chapter 6 walkthrough** involves the Neural Network Room. This is a massive 3D platforming puzzle where you must reroute power to the main elevator while being pursued by Chum Chompkins, who is now fully under the Prototype's control.

1.  **Lower the Bridge:** Use your Blue Hand to pull the lever on the far left balcony.
2.  **The Color Code:** You will see four monitors displaying a sequence. This sequence changes every playthrough. Note the colors and hit the corresponding pressure plates in order.
3.  **Avoid the Gaze:** Chum Chompkins hangs from the ceiling. If his spotlight hits you, it’s an instant fail. Hide behind the large toy crates to stay out of sight.
4.  **The Final Connection:** Once all three nodes are powered, use the Bio-Link to jumpstart the elevator motor.

> **⚠️ Warning:** The floor in the Neural Network Room is electrified. Touching the ground while the power is "On" will result in immediate death. Always stay on the raised catwalks.

## Boss Encounter: The Prototype's First Form

Unlike previous chapters where the main villain only appears at the end, the Prototype is a constant threat in Chapter 6. About halfway through the walkthrough, you will face his "First Form"—a terrifying amalgam of metal and organic parts.

To survive this encounter, you must use the environment against him. The Prototype is fast but heavy. Lead him toward the hydraulic presses located around the perimeter of the room. When he lunges, dodge to the side and use your GrabPack to activate the press. Doing this three times will force him to retreat, allowing you to enter the Extraction Chamber where Poppy is being held.

## Survival Tips for the Deep Factory

*   **Listen for Mechanical Whirring:** The Prototype’s limbs make a distinct grinding sound. If the volume increases, hide immediately.
*   **Conserve Your Flares:** You only get a limited number of flares in Chapter 6. Save them for the "Pitch Black" segments in the lower sewers.
*   **Check Every Locker:** Lore notes and battery packs are often hidden in lockers. These are essential for keeping your GrabPack's flashlight charged.
*   **Interact with Dr. Willard:** Whenever you see the Willard toy, interact with him. He often points toward hidden shortcuts that bypass difficult enemy encounters.
