## The Doctor’s Redemption and the Negation Formula

The return of Harley Sawyer—or at least a digital backup of him—changes the dynamic of the game entirely. While the "original" Doctor was destroyed in the labs, this AI version claims to be our ally. A prominent **poppy playtime chapter 6 ending theory** suggests that Sawyer isn't helping us out of the goodness of his heart, but out of a desire for revenge against the Prototype who betrayed him.

Sawyer’s primary goal in Chapter 6 will likely be to find a physical body. Theories suggest we may transfer his consciousness into an "Endo" frame or even the underutilized Globy character. Once mobile, the Doctor will be essential for crafting the **Negation Formula**. This chemical compound, mentioned in Chapter 5 documents, is rumored to be the only thing capable of breaking down the organic and mechanical bonds of the Prototype.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not fully trust the Harley Sawyer AI. His primary motivation is his own survival and the "credit" for his scientific discoveries, which may lead to a secondary betrayal at the end of the chapter.
