## The Origins of Rory Redcape

Before the downfall of Playtime Co., Rory was a flagship toy meant to inspire courage. Marketed with a catchy jingle and a "brave little prince" persona, he was sold as the ultimate guardian against the monsters under the bed. The **rorry red cape** was his most iconic feature, described in marketing materials as a "never-ending cape" that could fly at any pace and harden to protect the wearer.

## Character Abilities and Mechanics

When encountering the **rorry red cape** in Chapter 6, players must be aware of his versatile moveset. Unlike previous antagonists who rely solely on brute force or stealth, Rory utilizes his gear in a tactical manner. His cape is his primary weapon and tool for mobility.

### The Hardening Cape
The cape can change its physical properties instantly. It can stretch to reach across large chambers or harden into a spear-like state. During chase sequences, players should watch for the cape's color to deepen, indicating an impending strike.

### The Cookie Shield
While it sounds whimsical, the shield is a formidable obstacle. In gameplay, Rory uses the shield to deflect projectiles or environmental hazards that the player might try to use against him. Breaking his guard requires timing and the use of specific industrial tools found within the level.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt to hide in small spaces when the cape is active. Its "stretchy" nature allows Rory to reach into vents and crevices that were previously considered safe zones.

## FAQ

**Q: Is the rorry red cape a hero or a villain?**
