## The "Red Light, Green Light" Mechanic

A major highlight of the **poppy playtime chapter 6 gameplay preview** is the "Red Light, Green Light" sequence. This mechanic is tied to Lily’s singing. When she sings, the player is free to move and progress along the path toward the "prize." However, the moment she stops singing and goes quiet, the player must freeze perfectly still.

Failure to stop results in an immediate lose condition. This tension is amplified by the environment, which is often cluttered, making it difficult to find a clear path quickly. Players must balance the need for speed with the absolute necessity of staying still when the audio cues vanish.

### Survival Tips for the Singing Sequence:
1. **Listen for the Tempo:** Lily’s singing tempo may change. A faster song might mean a shorter window to move.
2. **Visual Cues:** Watch Lily’s braided limbs. They often twitch or tense up just before she stops singing.
3. **Pathfinding:** Identify your next piece of cover before you start moving during the "Green Light" phase.
4. **Don't Cheat:** As Lily mentions in the preview, she knows when you're hiding or trying to bypass the rules.
