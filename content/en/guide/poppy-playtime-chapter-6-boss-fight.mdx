## Preparing for the Final Encounter

Before entering the final arena, players must ensure they are familiar with the dual-hand mechanics introduced in previous chapters. The Chapter 6 arena is a massive stage rig, filled with high-voltage cables, water filtration systems, and vertical hazards. Unlike previous bosses who relied on speed, the Prototype uses psychological warfare and environmental manipulation.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Ensure your GrabPack is fully charged before triggering the initial cutscene. The first phase of the fight requires immediate mobility to avoid the Prototype’s initial sweep.

## Phase 1: The Stage Rig and Water Systems

The fight begins in the Theater District, where the Prototype has cornered the group. The initial goal isn't to damage the Prototype directly—his metallic chassis is too durable for standard tools. Instead, the strategy involves a high-risk "Fry and Soak" maneuver.

The Prototype will attempt to snatch Lillian (or other key NPCs) while taunting the player about ownership of the factory. During this phase, you must navigate the second-floor catwalks. The Prototype's claws will frequently smash through the floorboards, requiring you to use the GrabPack to swing across gaps.

### Activating the Sprinklers

To make the Prototype vulnerable, the entire stage needs to be soaked. This requires a three-step activation process:

1.  **Locate the Utility Room:** Navigate to the lower level while Huggy keeps the Prototype busy.
2.  **Reset the Shutoff Valve:** The Prototype will have jammed the main water line. Use the Blue Hand to wrench the valve open.
3.  **Engage the Emergency Lever:** Head to the second floor to pull the main power lever, which bypasses the dead electrical system.

| Step | Action | Hazard |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Reach the Utility Room | Catwalk collapses and Prototype swipes. |
| **2** | Turn the Valve | Steam vents that cause chip damage. |
| **3** | Pull Emergency Lever | High-altitude platforming and verticality. |

## Phase 2: The High Voltage Trap

Once the water system is active, the arena will begin to flood. This is the most dangerous part of the **poppy playtime chapter 6 boss fight**. You must remain on high ground while the Prototype is slowed down by the rising water levels. The Prototype’s metal components make him highly conductive, but a simple spark won't be enough to put him down.

You must retrieve the high-voltage cable from the stage rig's ceiling. This requires a series of precision jumps. If you fall into the water once the cable is live, it results in an instant game over.

### Managing the Live Cable

The live cable must be dropped into the floodwater at the exact moment the Prototype is standing in the center of the stage. 

*   **Timing:** Wait for the Prototype to roar and raise his central claw.
*   **Targeting:** Aim for the water surface directly beneath his feet.
*   **Execution:** Release the cable and immediately use the Purple Hand to propel yourself to the safety of the upper rafters.

> **💡 Tip:** Listen for the audio cue of the Prototype screaming about "dessert." This indicates he is locked into an animation and is most vulnerable to the electrical trap.

## Phase 3: The Cold Storage Escape

After the Prototype is significantly damaged by the electricity, he will retreat toward Cold Storage, specifically Locker C9. This transition phase is a timed gauntlet. You must follow him while avoiding falling debris and malfunctioning animatronics that the Prototype has "reanimated" with his remaining power.

The Prototype will attempt to use Lillian as a shield or a bargaining chip. In this final stretch of the **poppy playtime chapter 6 boss fight**, the player must use the Green Hand (if available in your 2026 build) to absorb residual electricity from the stage and fire it at the locker's locking mechanism.

## Survival Tactics and Best Practices

To successfully navigate the complexities of this encounter, keep the following strategies in mind:

*   **Avoid the Heights if Unprepared:** The Prototype thrives on knocking players off ladders. Always check the ceiling before committing to a climb.
*   **Protect Kissy Missy:** If Kissy is left in the open, the Prototype will prioritize her, leading to a "Mission Failed" screen. Ensure she is tucked behind the corner of the stage rig.
*   **Heart Rate Management:** The game features a stress mechanic during this fight. If your character's heart rate gets too high (indicated by the pulsing screen), hide in a locker for 5 seconds to regain composure.
*   **Environmental Cues:** Look for the red emergency lights. These always point toward the next objective lever or valve.

## FAQ

**Q: Can you defeat the Prototype without the water system?**

A: No, the water system is a mandatory mechanic of the **poppy playtime chapter 6 boss fight**. The Prototype's armor is immune to standard GrabPack shocks unless he is grounded in water.

**Q: What happens if Huggy Wuggy gets caught by the Prototype?**

A: If Huggy is caught, you have a short window to shock the Prototype's eye to force a release. If you fail, Huggy will be removed from the fight, making the subsequent phases significantly harder as the Prototype will focus entirely on you.

**Q: Is there a secret ending to the Chapter 6 boss fight?**

A: There are rumors of a secret ending triggered by collecting all 10 VHS tapes before the fight, which allows for a different interaction with the Prototype in Cold Storage, but this has not been confirmed for all platforms in the 2026 release.

**Q: How do I fix the "No Water" bug in the utility room?**

A: This usually happens if the emergency lever on the second floor wasn't pulled first. The water system requires power to pump, so ensure the lights are flickering red before trying to turn the manual valve.
