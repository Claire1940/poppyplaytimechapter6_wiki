## Navigating the Stage Rigging Puzzles

A major portion of **poppy playstation chapter 6** takes place in the theater and stage rigging areas. These sections emphasize verticality and require precise movement to avoid falling to a "nasty little ending." The primary objective here is to activate the fire suppression system to trap a metallic foe.

### Steps to Restore Power and Water

1.  **Ascend the Stage Rigging:** Use the ladders located on the left side of the stage. Be mindful of Rory’s warnings about heights, as certain platforms will collapse under sustained weight.
2.  **Locate the Utility Room:** The water shut-off valve is housed in a restricted utility area. You will need the GrabPack to pull the heavy lever.
3.  **Activate the Emergency Lever:** If the primary system is dead, head to the second floor to find the manual emergency bypass.
4.  **Flood the Stage:** Once the sprinklers are active, the floor will become conductive, setting the stage for the high-voltage trap.

## The Lily Boss Fight: Red Light, Green Light

The encounter with Lily in **poppy playstation chapter 6** is a psychological battle as much as a physical one. She utilizes a twisted version of "Red Light, Green Light" to hunt the player through her "house."

### Boss Mechanics Overview

| Phase | Lily's Action | Player Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Singing Phase** | Lily moves through the rafters | **Run** toward the next objective marker. |
| **Quiet Phase** | Lily stops and listens | **Freeze** immediately. Do not rotate the camera. |
| **Braided Strike** | Lily lashes out with her arms | Use environmental cover to break line of sight. |
| **The Prize Path** | Lily lures the player with "crumbs" | Follow the trail but keep the GrabPack ready for traps. |

When Lily goes quiet, the tension on the PlayStation DualSense controller haptics will increase. Use this sensory feedback to gauge how close she is to your position. If you move during the quiet phase, she will instantly grapple you, resulting in a game over.

## Tracking the Prototype via the Shield Trail

After the initial confrontation, the Prototype flees, leaving behind pieces of a shattered shield. Tracking these "cookie crumbs" is the only way to progress through the final act of **poppy playstation chapter 6**.

*   **Look for Blue Shards:** The Prototype’s shield sheds glowing blue fragments. These are often hidden behind crates or near side entrances.
*   **Side Entrances:** The Prototype’s massive frame prevents him from using standard doors. When the trail leads to a locked door, look for smaller vents or maintenance hatches that a human-sized player can fit through.
*   **Kissy Missy’s Recovery:** During the pursuit, you will find a detached arm belonging to the Prototype. This item is essential for the "Reattachment" side-quest which determines the ending you receive for Kissy Missy.

> **💡 Tip:** Don't rush the tracking sequences. The Prototype often leaves traps near the crumbs to catch impatient players.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I save Kissy Missy in poppy playstation chapter 6?**

A: To save Kissy Missy, you must successfully retrieve the Prototype's severed arm during the theater chase and bring it to the medical bay where Butcher and Chum are holding her. Failing to do this before the final boss encounter will result in her permanent incapacitation.

**Q: What happens if I move during Lily's "Red Light" phase?**

A: If you move during the "Red Light" phase, Lily will use her braided limbs to pull you into the rafters. On the PlayStation version, you have a split-second "Last Chance" QTE to use your GrabPack to stun her, but this only works once per encounter.

**Q: Where is the emergency lever for the sprinklers?**

A: The emergency lever is located on the second floor of the utility wing, behind a locked gate that requires a blue keycard. You can find the keycard in the "Guest Room" area where Lily first appears.

**Q: Is there a secret ending in poppy playstation chapter 6?**

A: Yes, if you collect all 12 "Cookie Crumbs" and successfully reattach the arm to Kissy Missy, you unlock a post-credits scene involving Huggy Wuggy’s return and a glimpse of the Prototype’s true face.
