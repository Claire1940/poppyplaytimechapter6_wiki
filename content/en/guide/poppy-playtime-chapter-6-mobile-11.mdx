## Mission Guide: The "Fry the Prototype" Plan

The core objective of the 11th gameplay segment involves a daring plan to use the Prototype's metallic structure against him. Because the Prototype is "half metal," he is susceptible to high-voltage electricity. However, a simple spark won't suffice; you need a massive surge combined with a conductive environment.

### Step 1: Initiating the Flood
To make the stage conductive, you must activate the fire suppression system. This is easier said than done, as the utility room is often locked or the power is cut.

1.  **Check the Utility Room:** Locate the shut-off valve. If there is no water flow, you must restore power.
2.  **Emergency Lever:** Head to the second floor. Be careful with the ladders, as the height can trigger "heart rate" mechanics that affect your movement speed.
3.  **Steady Hands:** When navigating the high beams, avoid slipping. If your heart rate gets too high, stay still for a moment to calm down.

### Step 2: The High Voltage Cable
While Butcher handles the water, you (or Rory) must retrieve the high-voltage cable from the stage rig. This requires climbing to the highest point of the theater.

| Action | Location | Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Retrieve Cable** | Stage Rig (High Ceiling) | Successful navigation of the ladder system. |
| **Activate Sprinklers** | Utility Room / 2nd Floor | Pulling the Emergency Lever. |
| **Drop Cable** | Main Stage | Timing the drop when the Prototype is standing in the water. |

## Tracking the Prototype: The Cookie Crumb Trail

After the initial confrontation on the stage, the Prototype will flee, taking "the shield" with him. Fortunately, he leaves behind a trail of "cookie crumbs"—small fragments of the shield that he sheds as he moves. Tracking these fragments is essential for finding Lily and progressing through **poppy playtime chapter 6 mobile 11**.

*   **Observation is Key:** Look for small, glowing fragments on the floor. They are often hidden behind crates or near doorways.
*   **The Side Entrance:** The trail will eventually lead to a locked house or stage area. The Prototype's massive frame cannot fit through standard doors, so you must locate the side entrance.
*   **Don't Touch Anything:** Once you reach the house, Rory will instruct you to stay by the door while he finds a way inside. Touching objects in this area can trigger environmental alarms.

## Mobile Optimization for Chapter 6

Playing **poppy playtime chapter 6 mobile 11** requires a device with at least 8GB of RAM to handle the advanced lighting and character models. If you experience lag during the high-voltage sequence, try the following optimizations:

1.  **Lower Shadow Quality:** The theater sequence has complex shadows that can tank framerates on older mobile devices.
2.  **Disable Motion Blur:** This helps with visibility when you are climbing the ladders and need to see the rungs clearly.
3.  **Sensitivity Settings:** Adjust your "GrabPack" sensitivity. The cable drop requires precise timing that is difficult with default touch controls.

> **Warning:** The "Locker C9" sequence in Cold Storage contains a jump scare that can cause a haptic feedback loop on some mobile devices. Be prepared for a sudden vibration.

## Survival Tips for the Prototype Encounter

The Prototype is more than just a physical threat; he is a psychological one. During the "Tea Party" dialogue, he will attempt to manipulate the characters by claiming ownership over everything in the factory, including Lillian (Lily).

*   **Stay Focused:** Don't let the dialogue distract you from the environment. Use this time to scout for the emergency lever's location.
*   **Huggy's Revenge:** When Huggy rips off the Prototype's arm, make sure to pick it up immediately. This item is required later to potentially "reattach" parts to Kissy Missy.
*   **The "Chump" Mechanic:** If you are playing as the "Chump" character, your primary job is proximity-based protection. Staying near Kissy Missy provides her with a defense buff.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the case in Poppy Playtime Chapter 6 mobile 11?**

A: The case is located in the Cold Storage area. You need to find Locker C9. Once opened, you will find the case inside which contains vital information regarding Lillian’s whereabouts.

**Q: How do I stop the Prototype from escaping the stage?**

A: You cannot fully prevent his escape in the current version of **poppy playtime chapter 6 mobile 11**. The goal is to "cook" him with the high-voltage cable, which forces him to drop the arm and flee, allowing you to track him via the shield fragments.

**Q: Is Kissy Missy gone forever if I don't move her?**

A: If you fail to move Kissy Missy behind the corner before the room crash, it results in a Game Over. You must restart the checkpoint and prioritize her safety before engaging the Prototype.

**Q: What are the "cookie crumbs" mentioned in the gameplay?**

A: These are fragments of the shield the Prototype stole. They act as a visual trail that leads the player toward the final house encounter where Lily is being held. Follow them closely to ensure you don't get lost in the dark hallways.
