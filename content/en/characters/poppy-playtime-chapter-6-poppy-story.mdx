## FAQ

**Q: Is Lily Lovebraids actually Poppy's sister?**

A: No, the **poppy playtime chapter 6 poppy story** clarifies that Lily Lovebraids is the toy version of a girl named Gracie, and Rory Red Cape is her biological brother. They are separate from Poppy’s original human identity.

**Q: How do I find all the Golden Statues in Chapter 6?**

A: There are five statues in total. The most difficult to find is the Prototype Statue, which is located on the train tracks during the final escape sequence. You must look for a small alcove on the right side of the tunnel.

**Q: Can you actually save Lily Lovebraids?**

A: Yes, but only through the secret Rory Red Cape ending. In the standard playthrough, she is crushed by the falling platform. Following the specific "tea party" and "platform jump" steps is the only way to trigger her rescue.

**Q: What does the Prototype want with Lily's hair?**

A: In the ending cutscene, the Prototype is seen snatching Lily's "weave" or hair. Lore theorists suggest he is collecting parts from every major toy to build a perfect, singular entity, which may be the final boss of the series.
