## Lily’s Abilities and Gameplay Mechanics

The **poppy playtime chapter 6 new character** introduces a high-stakes "Red Light, Green Light" mechanic that forces players to master precision movement. According to early gameplay footage, Lily utilizes her singing as a rhythmic cue for the player's survival. When she sings, you are permitted to move; when she falls silent, any movement results in an immediate and lethal strike.

### The Braid Reach
Because Lily’s limbs are composed of braids, she does not need to be physically close to the player to initiate an attack. She can anchor herself to the ceiling or distant walls, using her legs to swing across massive chambers in seconds. 

### Sensory Tracking
Lily appears to track "crumbs" or physical traces left behind by the player. In the narrative, Rory follows a trail of cookie crumbs and shield fragments, but this mechanic works both ways. If players linger too long in one area, Lily can triangulate their position based on the environmental damage caused by the player's GrabPack or movement.
