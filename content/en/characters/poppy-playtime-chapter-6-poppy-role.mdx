## Chapter 6 Gameplay Mechanics: Red Light, Green Light

One of the most intense segments involving the **poppy playtime chapter 6 poppy role** is the survival trial hosted by Lily. This sequence utilizes a "Red Light, Green Light" mechanic that tests the player's patience and reaction time.

1.  **The Singing Phase:** When Lily sings, the player is free to move toward the objective.
2.  **The Silence Phase:** The moment the music stops, the player must freeze. Any movement, including camera rotation, may trigger an attack.
3.  **The Prize Path:** Follow the designated trail to find "paint" or other key items required to satisfy the antagonist's demands and progress the story.

Poppy acts as your "ear" during these segments, often providing audio cues or warnings before Lily switches phases. Mastering this rhythm is essential for surviving the mid-game sections of Chapter 6.

## Navigating Lily’s House

*   **The Side Rooms:** Rory warns against poking around in side rooms, but Poppy often encourages exploration to find the lore tapes that explain how the Prototype convinced Lily to undergo her transformation.
*   **The Candy Trail:** To progress, you must use candy to lure out "it"—a yet-to-be-fully-revealed entity that Lily keeps as a pet or a guard.

> **Tip:** Always keep a surplus of "Sugar" items in your inventory. These are required for the candy trail puzzles and can buy you a few extra seconds during chase sequences.
