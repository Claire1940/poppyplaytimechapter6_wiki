## The "Unnamed Hundreds" and the Hour of Joy

The sheer scale of the **poppy playtime chapter 6 all characters list** is expanded by the "unnamed hundreds." These are the orphans who didn't become "Main" experiments like Mommy Long Legs or Catnap. In the aftermath of the 1995 Hour of Joy, these experiments were left to fend for themselves.

Many turned on each other as resources ran out. The environmental storytelling in Chapter 6 is expected to highlight the toy morgue and the vents where these incomplete creations still linger.
