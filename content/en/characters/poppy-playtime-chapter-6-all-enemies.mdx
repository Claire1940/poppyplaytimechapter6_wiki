## The Mystery of Experiment 1268

One of the most compelling pieces of evidence for a new primary antagonist comes from a Chapter 5 document detailing the conditioning of **Experiment 1268**. This male creature was subjected to intense psychological and physical restructuring using the "Poppy Gel" medium.

According to the logs, this experiment struggled with its identity, frequently muttering names like "Lewis" or "Lucy" before eventually succumbing to the conditioning. After 14 hours of treatment, the experiment was declared a success, with the creature repeating the haunting mantra: *"Joy, I live it. I feel it. I am it."*

A: The Prototype (Experiment 10006) is the primary antagonist. However, Experiment 1268 and a potentially corrupted Kissy Missy are expected to serve as major secondary bosses throughout the chapter.
