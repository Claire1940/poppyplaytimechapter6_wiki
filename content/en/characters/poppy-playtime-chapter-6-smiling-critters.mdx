## Smiling Critters in Poppy Playtime Chapter 6

The Smiling Critters were introduced in Chapter 3 as a group of colorful toy characters with a dark history. Their involvement in Chapter 6 builds on the tragic revelations of Chapter 3.

## Who Are the Smiling Critters?

The Smiling Critters are a group of animal-themed toy characters that served as a television show mascots before the events of the game. The group includes:

- **Catnap** — The purple cat, main antagonist of Chapter 3
- **DogDay** — The yellow dog, a central character in Chapter 3's tragedy
- **Bobby BearHug** — The bear character
- **Bubba Bubbaphant** — The elephant character
- **Craftycorn** — The unicorn character
- **Hoppy Hopscotch** — The rabbit character
- **KickinChicken** — The chicken character
- **PickyPiggy** — The pig character

## What Happened to the Smiling Critters

Chapter 3 revealed the terrible fate of the children who became the Smiling Critters. Most of their number was destroyed or corrupted by Catnap's influence.

## DogDay's Likely Role in Chapter 6

DogDay, who survived Chapter 3 in a severely compromised state, may have a continued role in Chapter 6 given his surviving consciousness and connection to the broader story.

## Catnap's Legacy

Despite Catnap's defeat in Chapter 3, his influence — particularly the red smoke he used to control creatures — may continue to affect the facility in Chapter 6.
