## The "Red Light, Green Light" Survival Mechanic

The **poppy playtime chapter 6 film** introduces a terrifying new gameplay loop centered around Lily’s "Stage." Drawing inspiration from classic playground games but with a lethal twist, players must navigate a path to a "prize" while Lily sings.

### Survival Steps for Lily's Stage:
1.  **Listen for the Song:** As long as Lily is singing, you are free to move toward the exit.
2.  **Freeze on Silence:** The moment the singing stops, you must become "perfectly, perfectly still."
3.  **Watch the Braids:** Lily’s limbs are made of braids that can reach across the entire room. If you move during the "Red Light" phase, her reach is instantaneous.
4.  **The Prize:** Reaching the end of the path rewards the player with a specialized "Shield" component, though the Prototype will attempt to steal this shortly after.

## Exploring the Deep Factory: Red Smoke and Mirrors

The lore of the **poppy playtime chapter 6 film** expands on the "Red Smoke" first introduced in earlier chapters. According to doctor recordings found in the 4K "True Ending" sequences, the Red Smoke was not just a hallucinogen but a tool for "translation"—moving subjects into a deeper, more dangerous part of the facility.

Players must navigate the Mirror Rooms, where experiments are kept behind one-way glass. These mirrors serve as a gameplay mechanic where the player must solve puzzles by looking at the "true" state of the room reflected in the glass, which often differs from the physical room they are standing in.

### Key Lore Locations in Chapter 6:
*   **The Mirror Gallery:** A hallway of experiments that react to your presence but cannot cross the glass... yet.
*   **The Doctor's Office:** Contains the final logs of the ranking officers who oversaw the Red Smoke translation.
*   **The Butcher's Lair:** A side-entrance area where the Prototype's "Butcher" keeps track of the "crumbs" left by intruders.
