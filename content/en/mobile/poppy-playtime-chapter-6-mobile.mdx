## The High-Voltage Stage Puzzle

One of the most challenging segments in **poppy playtime chapter 6 mobile** involves the theater stage confrontation. To defeat the Prototype in this sequence, you must use the environment against him. The Prototype is largely made of metal, making him susceptible to high-voltage electricity, but a simple spark won't be enough.

### Step-by-Step Execution

1.  **Locate the Utility Room:** Find the water shutoff valve. If the primary system is dead, you must head to the second floor.
2.  **Activate the Emergency Lever:** Located on the second floor, this restores power to the fire suppression system.
3.  **Soak the Stage:** Once the sprinklers are active, the entire floor becomes a conductor.
4.  **Drop the Live Cable:** Use your GrabPack to pull the high-voltage cable from the stage rig and drop it into the flooded area while the Prototype is standing in the water.

> **💡 Tip:** Don't rush the ladder climb. The Prototype can knock you off if you move while the stage is shaking. Wait for the "steady" audio cue before proceeding.

## Survival Tactics for the Mobile Environment

*   **Brightness:** Adjust the in-game gamma to 1.2. The mobile version can be significantly darker than the PC build, making certain interactable objects nearly invisible.
