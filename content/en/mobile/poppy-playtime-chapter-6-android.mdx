## Optimizing Performance for Mobile Horror

Playing a high-end game like poppy playtime chapter 6 android can lead to thermal throttling on many smartphones. To get the best experience:

*   **Disable Battery Saver:** This mode often throttles the GPU, causing stuttering during chase sequences.
