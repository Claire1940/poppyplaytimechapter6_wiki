## Developer Insights: The Isaac Updates

Recent communications from a developer known as Isaac have shed light on "what could have been" and what might still be coming. Isaac revealed that the original plan for the Doctor (Sawyer) involved a massive machine form that the player would have to dismantle from the inside out. While that specific boss fight was cut or altered, the concept of a "final confrontation" with a desperate, manipulative villain remains a core theme.

Isaac also confirmed a major lore point: **Ali is the Prototype.** For years, fans debated if the Prototype was simply imitating Ali’s voice to manipulate the player, but it is now confirmed that Ali is his true identity. This revelation changes the dynamic of the story, as it implies a deep, personal jealousy toward Poppy. Ali, likely an orphan adopted by Elliot Ludwig, felt overshadowed by the attention Poppy received, turning his childhood envy into a murderous obsession.

> **Warning:** The Prototype's ability to mimic voices is no longer his only weapon. Isaac revealed that the Prototype was always intended to have a "primary" voice, which we are likely to hear for the first time in Chapter 6.

Whether the player is a former high-level executive, a surviving experiment, or someone directly related to Elliot Ludwig, the truth will likely be the key to defeating the Prototype once and for all.
