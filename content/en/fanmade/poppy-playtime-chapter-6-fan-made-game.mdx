## Gameplay Mechanics: The High-Voltage Stage Puzzle

One of the most complex sequences in this **Poppy Playtime Chapter 6 fan made game** involves a high-stakes confrontation on a theater stage. To defeat the Prototype, or at least drive him back, players must utilize a combination of water and electricity. Because the Prototype is partially composed of metal, he is susceptible to high-voltage shocks, but a simple spark isn't enough.

Follow these steps to complete the Stage Rig puzzle:

1.  **Secure Kissy Missy:** Before starting the machinery, ensure Kissy is moved to a safe corner behind the stage.
2.  **Activate the Sprinklers:** Locate the utility room to find the water shut-off valve. If the power is out, you must manually override the system.
3.  **Restore Power:** Head to the second floor to find the emergency lever. This requires navigating narrow catwalks that are prone to collapsing.
4.  **Drop the Cable:** Once the floor is soaked, wait for the Prototype to enter the "flood zone" and drop the live high-voltage cable from the rig.

## Navigating the Cold Storage and Locker C9

A significant portion of the mid-game involves searching the "Cold Storage" wing of the factory. This area introduces a stealth-heavy mechanic where the player must avoid detection while searching for specific lockers. Locker C9 is a critical objective, containing a case that holds the key to Lily's whereabouts.

The atmosphere in Cold Storage is designed to induce panic. The Prototype often taunts the player through the intercom system, mentioning "tea parties" and "lovely guests," which hints at the grim fate of the missing children and toys.

### Survival Tips for Cold Storage
*   **Watch the Frost:** If your screen begins to freeze over, you have stayed in one spot for too long. Move to a heated vent to clear your vision.
*   **Listen for Metal Screeches:** The Prototype's frame is heavy. You can hear him moving through the vents long before you see him.
*   **Check the Shield:** The Prototype sheds pieces of a "shield" as he moves. These glowing crumbs act as a trail but can also lead you into dead ends.

> **💡 Tip:** Use the GrabPack to interact with lockers from a distance. This allows you to check for items without putting yourself in a corner where you could be trapped.
