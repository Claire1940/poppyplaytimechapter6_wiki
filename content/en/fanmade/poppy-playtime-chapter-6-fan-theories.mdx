## The Prototype’s Master Plan: Why Kidnap Poppy?

The most pressing question following the Chapter 5 finale is the Prototype’s motivation for taking Poppy. Unlike his previous victims, whom he killed or integrated into his mechanical mass, the Prototype specifically told Poppy, "I can fix you." This line has birthed several **poppy playtime chapter 6 fan theories** regarding the nature of the "Poppy Gel" and the search for a perfect vessel.

Many theorists believe the Prototype is struggling with biological degradation. While he can scavenge mechanical parts and organic tissue from fallen toys like Catnap and Mommy Longlegs, he lacks the stability of the original Poppy doll. Poppy was the first successful experiment, possessing a level of consciousness and physical integrity that later experiments lacked. By capturing her, the Prototype may be attempting to reverse-engineer her "perfect" formula to stabilize his own decaying form.

The revelation that Dr. Harley Sawyer exists as a digital backup within the factory’s data center changes the gameplay dynamic entirely for Chapter 6. Since the biological Sawyer was dispatched in Chapter 4, this "Digital Doctor" serves as a ghost in the machine.

This creates a "mutually assured destruction" scenario where Sawyer provides the player with high-level security clearance and map data in exchange for his continued survival.
